2007 Honda CR-V — Full Maintenance Project

Overview

A complete, hands-on mechanical project maintaining and repairing a 2007 Honda CR-V. This work spanned diagnostics, electrical troubleshooting, subsystem replacements, and routine maintenance — providing real, applied experience across vehicle systems.

What I Built

  • Performed full service: oil, filters, coolant, battery, belts, and fluids.
  • Replaced major components: power steering pump, AC compressor, condenser fan, alternator.
  • Completed electrical repairs including cruise control unit, AUX port, and door module.
  • Replaced suspension components like rear struts and hardware.
  • Used OBD scanners to diagnose, root-cause, and clear DTCs.

Key Outcomes

  • Developed practical systems-level understanding of vehicle components.
  • Improved diagnostic accuracy and troubleshooting speed.
  • Gained confidence in mechanical repair workflows end to end.

Tools & Technologies

  • OBD-II Scanners
  • Mechanic Hand Tools
  • Honda Service Manuals
  • Electrical Testing Tools
